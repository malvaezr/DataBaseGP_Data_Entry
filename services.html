Our Services

Comprehensive commercial steel erection, tilt-wall installation, and metal decking services.

01

Steel Erection

Our primary service and core expertise. We erect structural steel for commercial and industrial buildings of all sizes. With over 20 years of experience, our crews safely and efficiently assemble steel frameworks, columns, beams, and connections to bring your project's structure to life.

02

Tilt-Up & Precast Wall Panels

Tilt-up and precast wall panel erection is a key part of our scope of work. We safely and efficiently erect and install any amount of wall panels required by the project, including multi-story buildings. Our team ensures precise alignment and secure placement on every panel.

03

Metal Deck Installation

We offer metal decking installation on any project requiring it. Thanks to years of experience, we are very competitive in efficiency whilst loading, laying, and installing metal deck on any project, offering safe and quick installations to keep your project moving forward.

Our Commitment to Safety

At J & R Steel Welding, safety is our top priority and we will not cut corners. Although we hold an efficient and quick work ethic in high esteem, we will never sacrifice the safety of our workers for quicker completion times. Every project is completed to the highest safety standards in the industry.
